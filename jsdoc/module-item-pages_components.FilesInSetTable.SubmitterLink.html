Component: SubmitterLink¶

Show the submitter’s name and link to their profile. If passed a props.labName and props.showLabName == true, then will show that labName instead. Use for when hovering over the lab icon, for example.

Also shows props.labName if no props.user object supplied.

showLabName¶
