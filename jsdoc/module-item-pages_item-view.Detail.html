Component: Detail¶

Member Of Component: item-pages/item-view

A list of properties which belong to Item shown by ItemView. Shows ‘persistentKeys’ fields & values stickied near top of list, ‘excludedKeys’ never, and ‘hiddenKeys’ only when “See More Info” button is clicked.
