Component: FormattedInfoBlock¶

Member Of Module: item-pages/components

Formats a lab, award, or potentially other object to appear in a small rectangle that can be included in a sidebar. Also offers mix-in functions to help AJAX in the required details to the parent component’s state.

Also contains FormattedInfoBlock.List which is meant to display a list of FormattedInfoBlocks, and potentially AJAX in details for them.
